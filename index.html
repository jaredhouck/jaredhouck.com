About

Nursing leader. Informatics executive. Digital transformation partner.

I am a registered nurse and dual board-certified nurse executive and informatics leader with more than 20 years of experience advancing health system strategy, EHR optimization, AI governance, clinical workflow redesign, and clinician experience.

What I focus on

My work centers on improving patient care, reducing clinician burden, and helping healthcare teams use technology with discipline, clarity, and measurable purpose.

How I think about technology

Good informatics work starts before build. It begins with understanding the clinical problem, engaging the people closest to the work, and defining what success should look like.
