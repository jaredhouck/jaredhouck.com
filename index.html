About

Ideas I am working on

My current thinking centers on how healthcare can avoid using new technology to preserve broken workflows.

AI and nursing documentation

Before healthcare uses AI to help nurses document faster, we should ask whether the documentation is useful, necessary, and connected to patient care.

Documentation as a proxy

Healthcare often uses documentation because it is visible, not because it is the best measure of clinical practice or quality.

Problem-first informatics

The most important step in technology work is defining the actual problem before selecting a solution.

Smart rooms and clinical AI

Multi-sensor room technologies may become an important AI use case for nursing, patient safety, and virtual care models.
